I am a PhD candidate in the Wolfe Lab at Ohio State University. I study evolutionary genetics in the genus Penstemon looking specifically at the occurrence of polyploidy. Much of my work involves the development of statistical models to understand population genetic processes in polyploids, as well as trying to untangle the origins of polyploid taxa. All of my work is done in the genus Penstemon, which is an amazingly diverse group of plants in North America.
